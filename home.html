SERVICES

SOWA RIGPA

THE TIBETAN BOOK OF HEALTH

Sowa-Rigpa” is the traditional medicine of many parts of the Himalayan region used mainly by the Tribal and bhot people. Sowa-Rigpa(Bodh-Kyi) means 'science of healing' and the practitioners of this medicine are known as Amchi.

HOMEOPATHY

THE ORGANON OF THE HEALING ART

'Homoeopathy' was introduced as a scientific system of drug therapeutics by a German Physician, Dr. Christian Frederick Samuel Hahnemann in 1805. The principle of Homoeopathy is Similia Similibus Curentur.

UNANI

THE SCIENCE OF GRAECO-ARABIC MEDICINE

Unani System of medicine is a comprehensive medical system, which provides preventive, promotive, curative and rehabilitative health care. The system is holistic in nature and takes into account the whole personality of an individual.

Read More

SIDDHA

A HANDBOOK OF TRADITIONAL REMEDIES

The Siddha system is ancient system of medicine in India. The word 'Siddha' is derived from the root word 'Citti" meaning attainement of perfection, eternal bliss and accomplishment.Siddha is the mother medicine of ancien peninsular South India

Ayurveda

An Ancient Indian System of Medicine

Ayurveda involves ancient practices from India to heal and prevent disease while keeping the body and spirit in harmony.The term 'Ayurveda' meaning 'the knowledge of life' comprises of two Sanskrit words viz 'Ayu' meaning 'Life' and 'Veda'.

YOGA AND NATUROPATHY

SUTRAS OF PATANJALI

The word 'Yoga' comes from the Sanskrit word 'yuj' which means 'to unite or integrates'.Yoga is about the union of a person's own conciousness and the universal consciousness. Naturopathy is a holistic approach to wellness.
