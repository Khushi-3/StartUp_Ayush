ABOUT US

Get to know Startup India and Ayush

The Ministry of AYUSH is an association focused on propagation of traditional medicine. The All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA) is an Autonomous Institute under Ministry of Ayush.
It aims to harmonize the age-old wisdom of Ayurveda with modern tools and technology. In collaboration with Startup India,AIIA wants to identify and support innovations operating in the Ayush sectors,leveraging the potential of cutting-edge technologies.
Startup India is a flagship initiative of the Government of India, intended to catalyse startup culture and build a strong and inclusive ecosystem for innovation and entrepreneurship in India.

THEMES FOR CHALLENGES

AYUSH Food Innovations

In this era of the pandemic, a significant portion of the population is seeking ways to incorporate healthier meals into their lives while juggling increasingly busy schedules. There is a growing demand for convenient, ready-to-eat foods that require minimal preparation or cleanup. Innovations in the food delivery sector are playing a crucial role in meeting this demand, enabling consumers to maintain a healthier lifestyle even amidst their hectic work routines.

AYUSH Bio-Instrumentation(Hardware)

Innovations encompass a wide spectrum, ranging from 3-D printed devices for Ayurvedic medical technology products to cost-effective convenient and urgent care, telehealth solutions, Panchakarma instrumentation, advancements in laboratory-scale equipment for processing Ayurvedic pharmaceuticals, the introduction of drug ATMs, and instrumentation innovations aimed at enhancing the analysis and quality control of Ayurvedic medications.

AYUSH IT Solutions(Software)

Information technology innovations hold the remarkable potential to empower diverse stakeholders within the Ayush sector. These innovations span a wide array of areas, from leveraging Artificial Intelligence for Ayurveda diagnostics, harnessing the capabilities of virtual reality, utilizing bio-sensors and trackers, offering cost-effective convenient care, to optimizing the use of data to enhance physician experiences during diagnosis and treatment, including patient feedback. Additionally, innovations encompass database management, methods for identifying different phases of pharmaceutical processing, and the establishment of web-based networks for tracking raw drug consumption in the pharmaceutical industry, along with interlinkages.
